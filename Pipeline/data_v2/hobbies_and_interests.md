## Hobbies & Interests

### Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
Vaughn has been training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu since November 2020. He primarily trains No-Gi but enjoys returning to Gi training periodically. He has competed in a handful of tournaments, most recently at a Newbreed competition in 2025 where he won both the Intermediate and Advanced divisions.

### Golf
Vaughn has been golfing on and off since childhood and plays regularly in Hilton Head and Charleston. He maintains a 15-16 handicap and frequently golfs with his dad and friends when visiting family in Hilton Head.

### Cooking
Vaughn began taking cooking seriously in 2019 as a college freshman missing his mom's cooking. He started cooking for his dorm neighbors and learned through trial and plenty of error. He enjoys learning different cuisines and authentic foods and techniques, using them as interchangeable building blocks in other dishes. Given his heritage, he particularly loves classic Italian cooking including pizza, bread, and pasta from scratch.
